What is Gavel Club?

Gavel Club is an organization in which members between elementary and high school give both prepared and impromptu speeches, lead others, and develop their presentation skills. Gavel club is great for anyone who wants to be a confident public speaker, or break out of their shell. Gavel club is a copy of Toastmasters International, but is for children instead of adults.
